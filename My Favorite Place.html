    • My Favorite Place

    My favorite place is Sugarloaf Mountain in Carrabassett Valley, Maine. I grew up ski racing, and this was always my favorite place to go. When I go there, I feel as happy as I can be. I feel like I am home. My favorite restaurant is located on the mountain, The Bag. One of my favorite activities is going to get a bag burger after a long day of skiing.
