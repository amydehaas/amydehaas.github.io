    • My College Experience

    My college experience is different than most people's. I started my first semester of college at Roger Williams University in Bristol, Rhode Island. I loved that the school was on the ocean, but I did not fit in with the people and the small school atmosphere. Around November I decided that I wanted to transfer to the University of Maine. I am so grateful now that I made that decision, because I am happier than I've ever been before here.
